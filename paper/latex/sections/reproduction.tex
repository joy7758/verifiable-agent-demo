\subsection{Human-review smoke-test note}
\label{sec:appendix-human-review}

The checked-in human-review sheet is synthetic and is included only to verify that the blinded bundle workflow, answer key, and summarizer operate end to end. The synthetic summary covers eight blinded bundle rows, split evenly between baseline and evidence-chain conditions. Both conditions reach question accuracy 0.9375 and mean confidence 3.5; mean review time is 117.5 seconds for baseline bundles and 161.5 seconds for evidence-chain bundles. These values are not used in the paper as usability evidence and should not be interpreted as a real reviewer study.
